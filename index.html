About KV

Kendriya Vidyalaya (KV) is a network of central schools under the Ministry of Education, Government of India. We aim to provide a nurturing learning environment that promotes academic excellence and character development.

Our mission is to offer high-quality education accessible to all, and our values are rooted in inclusivity, innovation, and integrity.

Programs Offered

  • Science and Technology: Labs, robotics, STEM projects.
  • Languages and Literature: English, Hindi, Sanskrit, regional languages.
  • Art, Music, and Culture: Dance, painting, classical music, and theatre.
  • Sports and Leadership: NCC, scouts & guides, team sports, yoga, and fitness.
